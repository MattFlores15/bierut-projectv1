MICHAEL BIERUT

An acclaimed American graphic designer, critic, and educator. Renowned for his thoughtful, concept-driven approach to visual communication, his designs are recognized globally for their clarity and impact.

From his early work at Vignelli Associates to his partnership at Pentagram, Bierut has created iconic visual identities for clients ranging from cultural institutions to global corporations. He is a leading voice shaping contemporary visual communication.
